Inspect the cable path, not just the device.

iUSBDebugger is a macOS utility for hardware-focused inspection of USB, USB-C, USB Power Delivery, Apple mobile device connectivity, and Thunderbolt / USB4 topology.

Thunderbolt / USB4 topology

Conservative connector inference

Made for bench work.

Built for debugging real cables, hubs, chargers, docks, and ports on macOS with practical visibility into negotiated behavior.

Platform

Supports macOS 14.0 or later. The app is designed for real connected hardware and is most useful when diagnosing active USB, USB-C, and Thunderbolt / USB4 setups.

Use Cases

  • Verify whether a USB-C cable exposes EMCA identity information.
  • Check whether a charger or dock is visible on the USB-C control channel.
  • Inspect Thunderbolt / USB4 external topology and negotiated link behavior.
  • Review host controller ancestry and routing details during hardware debugging.
