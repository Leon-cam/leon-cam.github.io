Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Overview

iUSBDebugger is a local macOS utility for inspecting connected hardware topology and cable-related system information. The app is designed to analyze device information available from macOS system frameworks on the local machine.

Data Collection

iUSBDebugger does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data from within the app.

Local Device Information

The app reads local hardware-related information exposed by macOS in order to display USB, USB-C, USB Power Delivery, Apple mobile device, and Thunderbolt / USB4 diagnostics. This information is processed locally on the device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not require third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services as shipped.
